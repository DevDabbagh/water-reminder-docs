Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Water Reminder App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information via Authentication: If you sign in using Google or Apple, we collect your email address and basic profile information solely for the purpose of account authentication and identifying your user record.

Health Data: We store the water intake data you voluntarily enter into the app (amount and timestamp). This data is stored securely in our database linked to your account (or anonymous ID).

Device Information: We may collect standard crash reports and performance analytics to improve the app stability. This data is anonymized.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service (tracking your hydration).
  • To manage your account and authentication.
  • To send you push notifications for water reminders (local notifications).
  • To improve our App functionality and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies or unique device identifiers to serve relevant ads. We also use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage trends.

5. Guest Mode

If you use the app as a Guest, your data is stored securely but linked to an anonymous identifier. If you clear app data or uninstall the app, this data may be lost unless you link it to a Google or Apple account.
